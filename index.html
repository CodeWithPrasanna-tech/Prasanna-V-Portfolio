Prasanna V is an ambitious BCA graduate operating at the intersection of Artificial Intelligence, Data Science, and Embedded Systems, where code meets circuitry and data drives decisions. With a strong command of Python and C++, he architects end-to-end intelligent solutions that transform raw inputs into actionable intelligence.

His work spans microcontroller programming with ESP32 and Raspberry Pi, real-time computer vision pipelines using OpenCV, and IoT ecosystems that enable continuous monitoring and automation. Rather than treating AI as an abstract concept, he implements it at the edge, bringing intelligence closer to devices for faster, more efficient responses.

He is deeply focused on system optimization, low-latency processing, and scalable design, ensuring that every solution is both technically robust and practically deployable. His projects reflect a consistent theme: bridging the gap between physical systems and intelligent software to solve real-world problems.

With a mindset grounded in experimentation and iteration, he continuously explores emerging technologies in machine learning, robotics, and data analytics. Beyond development, he values clean architecture, test-driven thinking, and maintainable code. Prasanna is driven not just to build systems, but to engineer experiences that are intelligent, reliable, and future-ready.
